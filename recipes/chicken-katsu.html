Recipe For Chicken Katsu

Back to recipes!

Chicken katsu is a Japanese-style fried chicken. It's a thin juicy slice of chicken and explodes with flavor when you bite into it! Served alongside white rice and tonkatsu sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves.
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • Oil (preferablly neutral oil such as canola or vegetable)

Steps

  1. First season the chicken on both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Next, place flour, beaten egg, and panko crumbs into separate shallow dishes.
  3. Coat the chicken in the flour, shaking off excess, then dip into egg and finally press into the panko crumbs until well coated on both sides.
  4. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high hear.
  5. Place the chicken in the hot oil and fry until golden brown, 3 or 4 minutes per side.
  6. Transer to a paper towel lined plate to drain and then enjoy!
