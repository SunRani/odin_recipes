Vietnamese Beef Pho

What is Pho?

One of the lightest yet flavorful soups in the world (in my opinion). Pho is a Vietnamese soup dish consisting of broth, rice noodes, herbs, and meat and sometimes chicken. It originated in Northern Vietnam and has been popularized throughout the world!

Ingredients

  • 3 lb brisket
  • 2 lb meaty bones
  • 2 lb marrow bones
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Cinnamon
  • Cardamom
  • Corinander
  • Star anise
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Fish sauce
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Give the meat a quick 5 minute boil to remove impurities.
  2. Remove all scum that comes from the meat.
  3. Take the bones out and give them a good wash to remove all the scum.
  4. Place the bones, beef, onion, ginger, and other spices together in the same pot, then fill with water and bring to a light boil. Simmer for 3 hours.
  5. Remove the brisket and contiue to simmer with the bones for 40 minutes.
  6. Strain and then ladle into bowls with noodles and pile on toppings!
